Organisation Overview
o Name of the Organisation: Sukuma Sakhe Foundation (SSF) o History: Sukuma Sakhe Foundation is a community-based non-profit organization focused on improving the well-being of Umzimkhulu Municipality in KZN. The organisation works closely with local stakeholders to provide various support to local communities of Umzimkhulu. SSF achieves its goal through food drives, educational programs, health awareness and youth empowerment initiatives. Founded in 2020, the organisation has grown steadily. The foundation has played a key role in uplifting vulnerable groups through volunteer support and community partnerships o Mission: To empower communities by providing integrated support that improves the quality of life. o Vision: A self-reliant and thriving society where individuals have access to essential resources and greater opportunities to help them thrive for their dreams. o Target audience: Volunteers, sponsors, NGOs, donors, local community members and government partners.
